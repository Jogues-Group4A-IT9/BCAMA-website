Pearls Through a Sewer Pipe




Pearls Through a Sewer Pipe is a digital painting created by one of our members, Annie Palmiery, and is heavily inspired by The Source by Jean-Auguste-Dominique Ingres, and Girl With a Pearl Earring by Johannes Vermeer. It is made to represent the struggles of the Badjao Community to maintain their traditional form of livelihoods, fishing, and pearl gathering, amidst industrialization and urbanization.








Rhyne's Homicide




Rhyne’s Homicide is another painting created by Annie. It depicts the conflict of reason, morality, and consciousness, through the eyes of a forbidden love between a goddess who is cursed to kill whoever she touches, and a mortal who is so blinded by love, even towards a monster like her. The piece is heavily influenced by the sculpture Psyche Awakened by Cupid’s Kiss by Antonio Canova, and the song, Romantic Homicide by Dav4d. Art prints of this piece will be made to sell for our research mobilization in our third quarter.
