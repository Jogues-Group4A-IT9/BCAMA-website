PROFILING

The Profiling webpage was made in order to tell you all about the necesary information regarding the BCAMA background and its beneficiaries and benefactors. This is so that others can understand the BCAMA and the Badjao Community more.

The Institution's Background

The Badjao live by the shores of Borneo, Mindanao, Celebes and Indonesia, and as such, their main source of income would be fish. However, fish is not enough for the Badjao as other factors would come into play, such as the government restricting fishing spots to preserve fish, along with other environmental concerns such as typhoons and waste management. It would be this bad, that people from Badjao communities started looking for opportunities in cities, such as Manila, Davao, and etc. Thus, under the guidance of Pastor Felicisimo “Jun” Morales, the Badjao Community Association of Matina Aplaya Inc. was created. The person who founded the Badjao Community Association of Matina Aplaya Inc. was Pastor Felicisimo “Jun” Morales, a pastor who was interested in the Badjao. Pastor Jun wanted to help the Badjao and at the same time understand them, so he went to Isla Verde and with his wife, lived there for 8 years. He had gained the trust and respect of the Badjao community of Isla Verde; after much sincerity, he and his wife opened clinics. He had then founded the Badjao Community Association of Matina Aplaya Inc., in hopes of making the community a model for the Badjao community. He then left, making Ruben Ladyawan, a Badjao native and self taught artist, the chairman of the Badjao Community Association of Matina Aplaya Inc.

Beneficiaries and Benefactors

The beneficiaries of the Badjao Community Association of Matina Aplaya Inc. are the Badjao. There are many other types of Badjao subgroups all across the shores of Borneo, Mindanao, Celebes and Indonesia, but the people that come to the Badjao Community Association of Matina Aplaya Inc. are mainly nearby. Some of them might be from the Samal or East Coast Bajau subgroups. The beneficiaries are not just Badjao but people, who are in dire need of help, and want the best for themselves and their families and by the efforts of the Badjao Community Association, they are able to find the help and necessities that they need. There are two known primary benefactors, mainly the local government and the Ateneo de Davao University. The local government, as the government, is mainly responsible for them, the local government provides the necessary help needed to the Badjao Community Association of Matina Aplaya Inc., such as helping in providing healthcare, education and family planning. The Ateneo de Davao University, as its main mission to help those in Mindanao, aims to help the Badjao Community Association of Matina Aplaya Inc. in their goal of helping Badjao communities. The Ateneo de Davao University would initiate projects such as Project GypSEAS, a project focused on teaching Badjao communities their own cultural heritage, teaching adults agriculture and educating children.
