About the site

This website is all about spreading awareness to the Badjaos who have been suffering. From what we have seen, the main problems that we saw in the Badjao Community in Matina Aplaya is that the Badjaos there are uneducated in some prospects and they want their children to get educated on how to do business in order to make money. The other problem is cleanliness, the place surrounding the Badjao Community in Matina Aplaya has trashes on the pathway and on the side of the streets.
